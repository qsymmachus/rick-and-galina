While You're In Town

Rick's Pick

The Cleveland Indians are going to defeat the Baltimore Orioles at home on May 27th and May 29th. Tickets can be purchased online and are often available at the box office on game day. Don't miss this chance to watch the Indians battle it out for third place in the AL Central.

Galina's Pick

The Cleveland Museum of Art is a world-class institution with free admission every day. Tickets to the special exhibition Pharaoh: King of Ancient Egypt can be purchased online and advance tickets are recommended. Don't miss Gallery 222 - that's where they keep all the good stuff.
